Sheet= lw 	functional response trials of Chrysoperla rufilabris	

date		the month, day, and year that each trial was performed
predator	the predator under investigation: lacewing
cmbslifestage	the life stage of crapemyrtle bark scale (CMBS) used in the trial: either ovisacs, eggs, or crawlers
density		the number of CMBS in the petri dish before the trial began
replicate	the number assigned to a given petri dish to distinguish replicates from one another
eaten		the number of CMBS that had died after 24 hours of exposure to predator

notes:
AC= active crawler
